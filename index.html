AI Based househelp service

Our Vision

Our vision is centered around "Financial Inclusion for BHARAT," and to realize this goal, we have created a unique hybrid model that combines consumer technology(consumer-tech) with financial technology (fin-tech). Currently, we are tackling the pressing issue of non-availability of domestic househelps in India using Generative AI and Machine Learning.

Through our predictive analytics-driven platform, we can instantly locate maids and cooks in a user's vicinity, making the process highly efficient and user-centric. This consumer-tech aspect ensures that customers can book skilled professionals tailored to their specific preferences and requirements, all powered by ChatGPT 3.5 LLM.

Our Aim

Beyond our consumer-tech roots, we are also venturing into financial technology by developing a credit enablement platform. This platform utilizes Generative AI and leverages the Government of India's OCEN framework (Open Credit Enablement Framework).

By doing so, we are forging a dependable bridge between lending providers and underprivileged individuals, such as maids, in need of financial support. This fin-tech component plays a vital role in addressing the significant $330 billion untapped market within the highly disorganized domestic service sector.

In summary, our innovative approach merges cutting-edge consumer-tech capabilities, driven by Generative AI and machine learning, to provide real-time access to skilled professionals. Simultaneously, we are harnessing fin-tech solutions, utilizing the OCEN framework, to empower underprivileged workers with small-scale loans, ultimately driving financial inclusion in BHARAT.
